South Austin Bass Club

A close group of fishermen fishing Central Texas lakes within a 1.5 hour drive from Austin.

We hold team tournaments on the last Sunday of each month (except May, November and December - due to holidays). Our focus is friendly yet competitive fishing at a low cost, where every member has a voice in club decisions.

Tournaments
  • Local Lakes
    Within 1.5 hours of Austin
  • Last Sunday Monthly
    Except May, Nov & Dec (holidays)
  • Low Cost Entry
    $50/boat (includes Big Bass)
  • 100% Payback
    All entry fees paid out to 1st, 2nd, 3rd + Big Bass
