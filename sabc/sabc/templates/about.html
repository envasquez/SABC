Who We Are

South Austin Bass Club is a close group of fisherman who fish Central Texas within a 1 to 1.5 hour drive from Austin, Tx. Our goal is to have monthly tournaments that are friendly yet competative. We have a points system that is cumulative for the year and offer annual awards for the following:

  • Top Six Anglers
  • Angler of the Year
  • Big Bass for the Year
  • Largest Individual Stringer
