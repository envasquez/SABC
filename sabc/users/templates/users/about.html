Who We Are

South Austin Bass Club is a close group of fishermen who fish Central Texas lakes primarily within a 1-2 hour drive from Austin. We fish the last Sunday of each month except in November and December.

Our goal is to have monthly tournaments that are friendly yet competitive. We have a cumulative points system and offer annual awards for achievement and recognition.
