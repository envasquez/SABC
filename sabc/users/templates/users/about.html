Who We Are

South Austin Bass Club is a close group of fisherman who fish Central Texas lakes primarily within a 1-2 hour drive from Austin. Our goal is to have monthly tournaments that are friendly yet competative. We have a cumulative points system and offer annual awards for the following:

  • Top Six Anglers
  • Angler of the Year
  • Big Bass for the Year
  • Largest Individual Stringer

Club Information Highlights
  • Annual membership dues are: $20 per year
  • We meet once a month to vote on: Lake/Ramp/Time for the monthly tournament
  • Tournament entry fees: $20 per angler (includes Big Bass)
  • Tournament are Team format - 10 fish limit (5 per angler) per boat
  • Points are kept for individual performance, prize money paid to the team
  • Payouts are based on number of boats

For more information, please our see our bi-laws
