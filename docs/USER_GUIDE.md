## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Register** in the navigation bar
2. Fill in your information:
   - First and last name
   - Email address (used for login and notifications)
   - Password (must be at least 8 characters with mixed case and numbers)
3. Click **Register**
4. Log in with your new credentials

**Note**: New accounts require admin approval for full member access. Contact a club officer if you need expedited approval.

### Logging In

1. Click **Login** in the navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**

3. Check your email for a reset link

## Your Profile

Access your profile by clicking your name in the navigation bar, then **Profile**.

### Viewing Your Profile

Your profile shows:
- Personal information (name, email, phone)
- Membership status
- Tournament history
- Monthly weight chart (showing your performance by month and year)
- Yearly statistics (total tournaments, total weight, averages)
- Best finishes and achievements

### Editing Your Profile

1. Click the **Edit** button
2. Update your information:
   - First name and last name
   - Phone number (format: (512) 555-1234)
3. Click **Save Changes**

**Note**: To change your email address, contact an admin.

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to your Profile
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update Password**

## Voting on Polls

Club members can vote on various decisions, most commonly tournament locations.

### Accessing Polls

1. Click **Polls** in the navigation bar (members only)
2. View active polls and closed polls

### Voting on Active Polls

1. Click on an active poll
2. Review the options (for tournament location polls, you'll see lake, ramp, and time options)
3. Select your preferred option
4. Click **Submit Vote**

**Important**:
- You can only vote once per poll
- Your vote cannot be changed after submission
- Only verified club members can vote

### Viewing Poll Results

After a poll closes:
1. The results become visible to all members
2. Winner is highlighted
3. Vote breakdown shows how many chose each option

### Tournament Location Polls

For monthly tournament votes, each option includes:
- Lake name
- Launch ramp
- Start time
- Weigh-in time

The winning option automatically creates the tournament.

## Tournament Results

### Viewing Results

1. Go to **Calendar**
2. Click on a completed tournament
3. View the results page

Results include:
- Final standings (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.)
- Individual weights
- Number of fish caught
- Big bass winner
- Points awarded

### Understanding Points

The SABC uses a points system for Angler of the Year standings:
- **1st Place**: 100 points
- **2nd Place**: 99 points
- **3rd Place**: 98 points
- And so on...

Points are cumulative throughout the year.

### Big Bass

The heaviest single fish of the tournament wins Big Bass. Requirements:
- Minimum weight: 5 lbs to qualify for payout
- If no fish meets the minimum, the pot carries over

## Angler of the Year Standings

View current standings at **Awards** in the navigation bar.

### Current Year Standings

The standings table shows:
- Current rank
- Angler name
- Total points
- Number of tournaments fished
- Average points per tournament
- Best finish

### Historical Awards

View past years' winners:
- Angler of the Year champions
- Big Bass of the Year
- Rookie of the Year
- Most Improved

### How AoY is Calculated

- Points from each tournament are totaled
- All tournaments count (no drops)
- Ties are broken by:
  1. Head-to-head results
  2. Total weight for the year
  3. Number of tournaments fished

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q: Why can't I see the Roster or Polls?**
A: These features are for verified members only. If you've registered but don't have access, contact an admin to verify your membership status.

**Q: How do I become a member?**
A: Attend a club meeting, pay membership dues, and an admin will update your account status.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

### Voting

**Q: Can I change my vote?**
A: No, votes are final once submitted.

**Q: Why can't I vote?**
A: Check that:
- You're logged in
- You're a verified member
- The poll is still open
- You haven't already voted

**Q: When do polls close?**
A: Poll deadlines are shown on each poll. Typically, tournament location polls close before the monthly meeting.

### Tournaments

**Q: How do I enter a tournament?**
A: Show up at the designated ramp before start time and pay the entry fee to the tournament director.

**Q: What's the entry fee?**
A: Standard entry fee is $25, broken down as:
- $16 to the prize pot
- $4 to big bass pot
- $3 to club fund
- $2 to charity

**Q: What are the fish limits?**
A: Standard is 5 fish. Summer months (Jun-Aug) may have reduced limits of 3 fish due to heat stress on fish.

**Q: What's the dead fish penalty?**
A: 0.25 lbs is deducted for each dead fish at weigh-in.

### Results & Standings

**Q: When are results posted?**
A: Results are typically posted within 24-48 hours after the tournament.

**Q: How can I see my tournament history?**
A: Go to your Profile page to see all your past tournament results and statistics.

**Q: What happens if there's a tie in standings?**
A: Ties are broken by head-to-head record, then total weight, then number of tournaments.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The site isn't loading correctly. What should I do?**
A: Try:
- Refreshing the page
- Clearing your browser cache
- Trying a different browser
- Contacting an admin if problems persist

**Q: My tournament results look incorrect. Who do I contact?**
A: Email the tournament director or a club admin with the specific issue.

## Club Rules Quick Reference

### Tournament Rules

- **Start Time**: Safe light (as listed in tournament details)
- **Weigh-in Time**: As specified (typically 3:00 PM)
- **Late Penalty**: 1 lb per minute late, DQ after 15 minutes
- **Boundaries**: Lake specific, announced at start
- **Legal Fish**: Largemouth, Smallmouth, Spotted Bass (12" minimum)

### Scoring

| Place | Points |
|-------|--------|
| 1st | 100 |
| 2nd | 99 |
| 3rd | 98 |
| 4th | 97 |
| ... | ... |
| Zero | 50 |

### Big Bass Rules

- Minimum 5 lbs to win pot
- Must be alive at weigh-in
- Only one fish per angler counts
- Pot carries over if minimum not met
